About Me

Lourds Richard Jeevith A

Senior PHP Developer & DevOps Engineer

I am a passionate and results-driven PHP Developer with 5+ years of hands-on experience in designing, developing, and deploying high-performance, scalable web applications. My core expertise lies in Laravel 12 and CodeIgniter 3 frameworks, combined with strong DevOps & Cloud infrastructure skills. I thrive on turning complex business requirements into elegant, efficient technical solutions that drive real business value. From architecting database schemas to deploying production-ready applications, I handle the full development lifecycle with precision and care.

I specialize in building scalable, secure, and maintainable backend systems, RESTful & GraphQL APIs, and microservices architecture. I have successfully delivered 50+ projects across diverse domains including e-commerce, healthcare, logistics, education, and SaaS platforms. My code follows SOLID principles, design patterns, and test-driven development (TDD) practices, ensuring long-term maintainability. I'm also proficient in database design, query optimization, caching strategies (Redis), and message queues for building robust, high-traffic applications.

On the DevOps side, I bring deep expertise in AWS cloud architecture (EC2, S3, RDS, Lambda, ECS, CloudFront), Docker containerization, Kubernetes orchestration, Terraform infrastructure-as-code, and Jenkins/GitHub Actions CI/CD pipelines. I've implemented automated deployment workflows that reduced deployment time by 70-80% and improved system reliability through blue-green deployments and rolling updates. I also manage Linux servers, Nginx/Apache configurations, SSL/TLS security, monitoring with CloudWatch/Prometheus, and log aggregation with ELK stack.

Beyond coding, I'm an active open-source contributor and passionate about knowledge sharing. I regularly write technical articles on Medium and Dev.to, contribute to PHP/Laravel community packages, and mentor junior developers to help them grow in their careers. I believe in continuous learning and stay updated with emerging technologies like serverless computing, edge functions, WebAssembly, and AI/ML integration. I've also conducted internal workshops on Docker, CI/CD best practices, and clean code architecture for development teams.

Currently, I'm focused on architecting cloud-native solutions and helping businesses modernize their legacy infrastructure into scalable, resilient microservices. I'm actively seeking exciting freelance projects, long-term collaborations, and remote opportunities where I can leverage my full-stack development and DevOps expertise to make a meaningful impact. If you have a challenging project or need a reliable technical partner, let's connect and build something amazing together! 🚀
